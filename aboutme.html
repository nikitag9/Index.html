About me

I'm from Bangalore, the IT capital of India and I'm currently in my third year at Georgia Tech. I work as a Teaching Assistant for an introductory Python class in the College of Computing. I'm also interning part-time with Home Depot as a User-Experience Design intern. Over the last summer(2022) I also interned with the company full time in the Finance UX team and worked towards re-designing and prototyping the process of creating a quarterly report that goes out to Wall Street. I'm currently also working on the Vertically Integrated Project(VIP) AccessCORPS as a front-end developer to work toward increasing accessibility in GT course material for students with impairments or disabilities.

I love working as a UX designer as it allows me to help make people's lives easier by using my love for art, psychology, and software design. In my free time I like experimenting with different art media, game design, graphic design, VR, and animation.
